## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of the revenue performance, pricing efficiency, and market trends for **Atliq Grand Hotels**. Using Power BI, I developed an executive-level dashboard to help management recoup market share lost to competition by optimizing pricing strategies and identifying expansion opportunities across major Indian cities.

## 📉 Problem Statement
Atliq Grands, a luxury hotel chain with a 20-year legacy, faced declining market share in the luxury and business segments. The goal was to move away from intuition-based decisions and implement a **data-driven revenue management strategy** using key hospitality metrics.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Power BI**: Dashboard development & interactive visualization.
* **Power Query**: Data cleaning, ETL, and transformation.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**: Created complex measures for KPIs like RevPAR, ADR, and Occupancy %.
* **MS Excel**: Primary data source.
* **Gamma AI & OBS Studio**: Used for professional presentation and recording.

## 📊 Key Hospitality Metrics (KPIs)
To measure performance accurately, I implemented the following industry-standard metrics:
* **RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room):** Measures the ability of a property to fill its rooms at a certain price.
* **ADR (Average Daily Rate):** The average rental income per paid occupied room.
* **Occupancy %:** The ratio of rented or used space to the total amount of available space.
* **Realization %:** Percentage of successful check-ins out of total bookings.

## 💡 Strategic Business Insights

* **🏆 Mumbai - The Powerhouse:** Generates nearly **2x more revenue** than other cities. High ADR and RevPAR suggest strong pricing power.
    * *Strategy:* Optimize premium pricing and consider further luxury expansion.
* **🚀 Delhi - The Growth Opportunity:** High demand and stable RevPAR, but lower total revenue due to fewer properties.
    * *Strategy:* Significant potential for new property launches to capture existing demand.
* **⚠️ Hyderabad - Operational Focus:** Currently underperforming with lower occupancy and customer satisfaction ratings.
    * *Strategy:* Improve service quality and operational efficiency before further investment.

## 📈 Project Outcome
This dashboard empowers stakeholders to:
1.  **Identify** underperforming markets instantly.
2.  **Optimize** pricing strategies based on ADR trends.
3.  **Allocate** marketing budgets toward high-performing booking channels.
4.  **Plan** data-backed expansions into high-demand cities like Delhi.
